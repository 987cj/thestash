# Compare the areas of one pizza vs two pizzas!
This was just a small, fun project made over the weekend. My dad wanted to know if buying one bigger pizza was better or worse than two smaller pizzas. I could've just brought out a calculator and called it a day, but something inside me bugged me to make an entire website for it!

I'm really happy with how this turned out - if you compare this to some of my earlier website work, it's a whole league better design-wise. Part of this has to do with my embrace of Tailwind, which has made me a lot faster with working with CSS, as well as now being a lot familiar with concepts of flexbox and animations. Considering that this was strung up in such a short amount of time, I think it's a great demonstration of my growth in that area.
